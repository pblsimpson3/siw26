Colonial Life Passport Assignment 2

Compare your life with the lives of the colonial children

Use your handout Comparison of a Colonial Child's Life to My Life. In addition to what you already know, you may use the information on the following site to fill in the comparison chart.

Go to this site and learn how
these children lived.

After completing the comparison chart, write an essay that answers the following questions: Was it difficult to be a child during colonial times? Why or why not? Use examples and give reasons for your ideas.

After completing the comparison chart, create a booklet comparing your life to the life of a colonial child. You may draw or use pictures for illustrations. Arrange the booklet to best compare the two lives.
