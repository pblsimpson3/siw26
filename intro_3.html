Before we begin, let me tell you a little bit about this exciting park.

You will need a park passport to travel this adventure. Get your passport from your teacher.

The Iron Works is part of the National Park System and is one of about 370 national parks and historic sites in the United States.

The Iron Works was started in 1646, just 26 years after the Pilgrims arrived in the New World. The founders of the Iron Works and their families had a great adventure in the New World. You will too!
