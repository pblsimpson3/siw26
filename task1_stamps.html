Boys and Girls, there are thousands of Park Rangers in the United States. They have many different and exciting jobs.

Today, you have a chance to become a Park Ranger!

Each time you complete a task have your teacher stamp your passport. With enough stamps...YOU will become a RANGER.

9 Stamps = SENIOR RANGER

7 Stamps = JUNIOR RANGER

5 Stamps = TRAINING RANGER

Receive an official Ranger Certificate and Ranger Badge
